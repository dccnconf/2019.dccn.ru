Registration

Papers should be submitted via registration system at https://dccn.ru (opens at 1 April 2019).

Please note that all co-authors must be registered and added as co-authors inside the registration system in order to submit the paper.

Russian citizens, students of Russian universities with co-authors:

3000 ₽

200 €
